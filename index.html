    Why choose Fujitsu ?

    We have the experience and expertise

    Fujitsu has been developing mission-critical enterprise database systems for over 35 years and has provided PostgreSQL development, product support, training and consulting services to large corporate and government organizations globally for more than a decade.

    We are leaders in open source development

    Fujitsu has been a leader in open source development since the early eighties. As a long-term contributor to the development of open source database systems, Fujitsu is proud of its commitment to the promotion of PostgreSQL as a world-class mission-critical enterprise database.

    We provide end-to-end solutions

    Fujitsu is the only vendor providing complete end-to-end PostgreSQL solutions with support, migration, hardware appliance and cloud offerings. We provide what you need, when you need it Fujitsu provides the support, services and training you need, when you need them.
